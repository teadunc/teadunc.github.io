About Green Earth Community Cleanup

Green Earth Community Cleanup is a volunteer organization dedicated to improving local neighborhoods through environmental projects. We believe that small actions from community members can create a lasting positive impact.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring people together to protect natural spaces, reduce pollution, and encourage environmental responsibility.

Our Goals

  • Keep public areas clean
    • Remove litter from parks
    • Protect local wildlife habitats
  • Teach sustainable habits
  • Create opportunities for community service
